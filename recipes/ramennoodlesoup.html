Ramen Noodle Soup

Quick and Easy Ramen Noodle Soup

Indulge in a comforting bowl of homemade ramen noodle soup that's not only quick to prepare but also bursting with flavors. This easy-to-follow recipe combines simple ingredients with minimal effort, making it perfect for busy weeknights or lazy weekends.

With its savory broth, tender noodles, and customizable toppings, this homemade ramen is sure to satisfy your cravings and warm you up from the inside out. Whether you're a novice cook or a seasoned chef, this recipe is guaranteed to become a staple in your culinary repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 medium carrots, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 bunch bok choy, chopped
  • 1 tsp chopped garlic
  • 6 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 2 tsps soy sauce
  • 30g uncooked ramen noodles
  • 4 eggs, boiled
  • red chilli flakes, garnish
  • sesame seeds, garnish
  • green onions, sliced

Directions

  1. In a large pot, heat the oil on medium heat.
  2. Add the carrots and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Then add the bok choy and garlic. Stir together.
  4. Pour the broth in and bring to a simmer. Add soy sauce and ramen.
  5. Cook for 4 minutes in the broth or according to package instructions. Make sure not to overcook or the noodles become soggy.
  6. Remove the pot from the heat and serve ramen soup immediately with egg, green onions, sesame seeds and chili flakes
  7. Store the leftovers in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
